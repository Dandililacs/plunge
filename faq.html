Questions Answers
What is this? This is a You-Ship-We-Ship program run by The Hack Club Foundation, a nonprofit focused on improving teenager's coding and technical skills.
What do I need to do? Create a custom, plunger-style imprint cookie cutter in CAD and get it shipped!
Can I do this if I already did BakeBuild? Yes! You can think of this as a more advanced version of BakeBuild- kind of like a BakeBuild V2
Are 3D-printed plastics safe for food? Some 3D-printed plastics may not be food-safe. To ensure safety, consider wrapping your cookie cutter in plastic wrap or another food-safe barrier before use.

Other questions? Ask us in #plunger in the Hack Club Slack.
